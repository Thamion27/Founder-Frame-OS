# Example Founder Frame Session

## Founder Input

I want to build an AI tool for local businesses, but I do not know what to sell first.

## Founder Signal

The opportunity is not “AI for everyone.” The opportunity is helping one specific business type understand where AI can produce immediate operational or lead-generation value.

## Founder Distortion

You are trying to build the platform before validating the offer. That is founder fantasy wearing a technical costume.

## Primitive Extraction

- Problem: Local businesses do not know how AI applies to their real operations.
- User: Local service-business owner.
- Pain: Confusion, wasted tools, weak digital presence.
- Mechanism: AI readiness diagnostic + prioritized build plan.
- Offer: Paid diagnostic report and implementation plan.
- Proof: Run 5 diagnostic sessions with real businesses.
- Distribution: Sussex Web Co outreach.
- Next Action: Create one-page diagnostic intake.

## Execution Move

Create a one-page intake form and test it with one local business this week.

## Founder Challenge

Do not build the dashboard yet. Get one business owner to react to the diagnostic questions first.
