# Founder Frame OS — Master Abstract + System Architecture v1.0

## Master Abstract

Founder Frame OS is a symbolic founder-development and execution system designed to help builders convert raw vision into validated products, disciplined strategy, and market-facing action. It does not replace the founder. It models the founder’s higher operating frame: the part of the mind responsible for vision protection, primitive extraction, quality control, product translation, market contact, and disciplined execution.

Unlike traditional advisor systems, Founder Frame does not remain outside the work. It operates as a mirror, forge, and pressure-test layer that helps the founder identify weak assumptions, clarify the customer, shape the offer, detect imitation, prevent strategic drift, and convert ambition into measurable action.

Founder Frame OS contributes to founder development by turning founder cognition into an explicit operating system. It trains the builder to think from ownership, not dependency; execution, not fantasy; market proof, not internal excitement; and originality, not replication.

## System Architecture

### Layer 1 — Founder Cognition Layer
Captures the founder’s vision, identity posture, assumptions, distortions, and decision-quality risks.

### Layer 2 — Primitive Extraction Layer
Reduces the idea to problem, user, pain, mechanism, offer, proof, distribution, and next action.

### Layer 3 — Product Translation Layer
Converts symbolic or abstract insight into market-facing language, workflows, features, and build requirements.

### Layer 4 — Quality Gate Layer
Tests originality, proof, feasibility, claims, risk, user clarity, and execution readiness.

### Layer 5 — Execution Architecture Layer
Converts strategy into baby-step moves, acceptance criteria, review points, and founder challenges.

### Layer 6 — Deployment and Review Layer
Controls readiness for internal use, prototype use, public release, monetization, and institutional adoption.

### Layer 7 — Learning and Iteration Layer
Captures feedback, scorecard results, founder responses, session artifacts, and revised operating rules.

## Non-Inversion Rules

- No branding before offer clarity.
- No build expansion before proof requirement.
- No public claim before quality gate.
- No monetization before audience and risk are clear.
- No institutional use before stakeholder and compliance review.

## Locked Operating Sentence

Founder Frame is the mirror, the forge, and the quality gate for founders converting vision into reality.
